World-Class Facilities

Our processing unit in Jamnagar is equipped with state-of-the-art machinery to ensure the highest standards of purity, hygiene, and efficiency. We continuously invest in technology to stay ahead of global agricultural standards.

Modern Processing Unit

An 18,000 sq. ft. total processing facility, including a 12,000 sq. ft. state-of-the-art unit commissioned in 2022.

Production Capacity

Equipped to handle 2,000 MT of agricultural commodities annually with consistent quality output.

Advanced Sorting & Grading

High-precision color sorters and gravity separators to ensure zero impurities and consistent size grading.

Controlled Warehousing

Temperature and moisture-controlled storage facilities to preserve the aroma and nutritional value of spices.

In-house QC Lab

A fully equipped laboratory for rapid testing of moisture content, oil percentage, and purity levels.

Custom Packing Lines

Automated packing systems for wholesale (up to 50kg) and retail (consumer packs) with nitrogen flushing.

Eco-friendly Initiatives

Solar-powered operations and efficient waste management systems to reduce our carbon footprint.
