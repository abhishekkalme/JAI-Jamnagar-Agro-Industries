Our History

Originally founded as a partnership firm under the name "Marshall Food Industries" in 2014, the business steadily expanded its operations and presence across South, West, and Central India. With a clear commitment to growth, the company has now evolved into a limited entity and is taking strategic steps to diversify its offerings.

2014

Established manufacturing operations with strong Central India presence

2018

Ventured into international agri-trading with onion exports and fruit imports

2020

Expanded into South India, building enduring client partnerships in 4 states

2022

Commissioned new 12,000 sq. ft. state-of-the-art manufacturing facility with latest machineries

2024

Scaled operations to direct container sales with merchant exporters

2025

Transformed into a Limited Company and launched retail product line for diversified growth
