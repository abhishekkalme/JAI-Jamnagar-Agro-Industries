Our Legacy & Vision

Jamnagar Agro Industries (JAI) traces its roots to a passion for agricultural excellence. Our journey is defined by a consistent pursuit of quality, from the local market to the global stage. Our vision is to become the world's most trusted partner for premium Indian spices and agri-products, driven by innovation and sustainable practices.

  • 2014

    Strategic Foundation

    Established under the name "Marshall Food Industries" with a focus on manufacturing high-quality spice blends and establishing a strong foothold in Central India.

  • 2018

    Global Horizons

    Ventured into international agri-trading, successfully initiating onion exports and fruit imports, marking our entry into the global supply chain.

  • 2020

    Pan-India Presence

    Expanded operations into South India, building enduring partnerships across 4 major states and diversifying our procurement network.

  • 2022

    Technological Leap

    Commissioned a new 12,000 sq. ft. state-of-the-art manufacturing facility in Jamnagar, equipped with the latest machinery for precision processing.

  • 2024

    Scalable Logistics

    Scaled operations to direct container sales with merchant exporters, streamlining our export logistics for maximum efficiency and reach.

  • 2025

    A New Era: JAI Limited

    Transformed into a Limited Company and launched our flagship retail product line, positioning JAI for a new era of diversified global growth.
