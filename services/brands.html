Retail Brands

SKP Raja Rani

A brand with heritage and evolution. Launched in 2011 as our retail offering, Raja Rani transitioned to wholesale in 2014, growing alongside our B2B expansion. Now in 2025, we're bringing it back to retail with comprehensive marketing support, combining 15 years of trust with fresh consumer focus.

  • Heritage & Evolution

    Launched in 2011, transitioned to wholesale in 2014, and returning to retail in 2025.

  • 15 Years of Trust

    Combining years of market presence with fresh consumer focus.

Exclusive Partnership

Ras Paan Mukhwas

Our exclusive partnership with Jamnagar's legendary mukhwas makers, Jamnagari mukhavas, a family business serving since 1980, now in its 4th generation. Together, we've created Ras Pan, blending their authentic Jamnagari expertise with our distribution strength to bring traditional mouth fresheners to a wider market.

  • Jamnagari Expertise

    Authentic recipes passed down through four generations since 1980.

  • Distribution Strength

    Leveraging our network to bring traditional flavors to a wider audience.

  • Team Designed
