Our Journey

Since our inception, Jamnagar Agro Industries has been at the forefront of the agricultural sector, evolving from a local supplier to a global powerhouse.

  • 2013-2015: Foundations

    Inaugurated our first processing unit and established the SKP Raja Rani brand, focusing on high-quality spices and seeds.

  • 2016-2020: Expansion

    Expanded our reach across Gujarat and Rajasthan, investing in state-of-the-art infrastructure and advanced sorting technology.

  • 2021-2023: Global Reach

    Launched the ‘Parampara’ brand and scaled operations to over 200 Crores in revenue, specializing in exports and white labelling.

  • Infrastructure

    Operating from a 20,000 sq ft state-of-the-art facility in Hapa Industrial Area, Jamnagar, ensuring the highest standards of hygiene and quality.
